Top 5 · Top Bars

Same heatmap, inverted rule: the top of the screen is the red zone, so a great top bar is glanceable, not tappable — status and context live here free of charge; primary actions up here cost a regrip. Every option below assumes the avatar/account moved into the bottom dock (#1 above), which frees the top bar from its worst job.

Verdict

Pairing: Floating Pill Dock (bottom) + Status Command Bar (top). Bottom owns every tap (all five targets in the green zone, avatar included); top owns every glance (availability state, live counts, bell). That split — act low, read high — is the whole heatmap lesson in one sentence, and no AU job platform does both today.

#1 Floating Pill Dock detail: it keeps everything you shipped today (4 work tabs + Schedule + account sheet) but relocates the avatar from the worst spot on the screen (top-right red zone) into the best (bottom dock), and gives Jobdun the most current visual silhouette of the five. #2 Centre Action Bar is the bold alternative if you want the AVAILABLE/POST JOB action to define the product. They can combine later: a floating dock with a centre action is a natural v2.
