ColorScheme.fromSeed(seedColor: action), then brand-critical roles pinned to exact, contrast-verified hex. Single-accent: secondary & tertiary reuse the orange.

Typography — Oswald + Open Sans

lib/app/theme/app_typography.dart (AppTypography.textTheme)

Oswald — display, headings, all-caps labels/buttons. Open Sans — titles, body, captions. ~1.2 minor-third ramp anchored at 16 (Google floor). Fixed logical px (never .sp). Role names are the M3 TextTheme roles.

Icon Sizes

lib/app/theme/app_icon_size.dart · lib/core/theme/app_icon_theme.dart

Material-aligned steps at true pixel size. Glyphs come from Phosphor via AppIcons.* — Bold = outline/inactive, Fill = active. Default & nav = 24dp.

Spacing — 4dp grid

lib/app/theme/app_spacing.dart (AppSpacing)

Six tokens cover the app. Need 12dp? Use md (16). Applied via Gap(AppSpacing.md.h) — never raw SizedBox.

Radius — sharp 4–8 band

lib/app/theme/app_radii.dart (AppRadius)

Aggressive-flat: nothing rounds past 8 (radius > 12 is a MASTER §238 anti-pattern). Buttons & inputs share 6.

Motion

lib/app/theme/app_motion.dart (AppMotion)

150–200ms, ease-out. No spring/bounce, no slow token — past 200ms reads as a SaaS loader. Tap a track to replay.
