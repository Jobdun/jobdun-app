TRADIE HOME · AVAILABILITY + MAP — 5 VARIATIONS

Five takes on combining #02 Availability Hero + #04 Map-First Jobs — the rideshare-driver model for trades ("I'm ON → here's the work near me"). They differ in how dominant the map is and where availability sits. Variation C is the all-important OFF state the whole pattern hinges on. Same Jobdun system: dark, orange CTA, Oswald + Open Sans, flat, dark-on-orange.

A

DRIVER MODE (full map)

Full-bleed map. A floating OPEN-FOR-WORK pill sits top-left; pins show pay; a draggable sheet peeks the matched job list. Most immersive — pure rideshare feel.
Strongest when distance is the #1 decision and you want the boldest, most modern home.
