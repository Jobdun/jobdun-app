# GALVANISED — Voice & Tone
**Australian Tradie Marketplace · v1.0**

---

## The Rule

Write like a builder talks to another builder on a job site. Direct. No filler. No excitement about things that aren't exciting.

---

## Do / Don't

| ✓ Write like this                        | ✕ Not like this                                      |
|------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Electrician available nearby             | Great news! A skilled professional is nearby!        |
| Job posted. 3 tradies notified.          | Your job is live! Let the magic begin 🎉              |
| Insurance expires in 14 days.            | Heads up! Your insurance might need a refresh soon.  |
| Marcus accepted your request.            | Exciting news — Marcus is on board!                  |
| No tradies available right now.          | Oops! No one's around right now, check back later!   |
| Licence verified.                        | This person has been fully vetted by our team!       |
| Start: Tomorrow 7am                      | Your job kicks off bright and early tomorrow!        |
| 4 tradies match your job.                | We found some amazing options for you!               |
| Payment failed. Update card details.     | Uh oh! Something went sideways with your payment!   |
| Job completed. Rate Marcus.              | Woohoo! Job done! How'd Marcus do?                   |

---

## Tone Principles

**Direct** — Subject, verb, object. No preamble.  
**Specific** — "3 tradies notified" not "several tradies notified"  
**Calm** — No exclamation marks. Competent people don't shout.  
**Honest** — No softening bad news. "Payment failed." not "Payment couldn't process."  
**Respectful** — These users are professionals. Treat them accordingly.

---

## Punctuation Rules

- No exclamation marks in system UI copy
- No emoji in buttons, badges, navigation, or form labels
- Em-dash (—) for ranges and separations, not hyphen
- Sentence case for all UI labels — not Title Case or ALL CAPS (exception: display headings)
- Numbers as digits: "3 tradies", not "three tradies"
- Distance: "3.2 km" with space before unit
- Time: "7am", "2:30pm" — no space, lowercase am/pm

---

## Brand Vocabulary

Use these words. They're how the industry actually talks.

**Preferred:**
- Tradie / Tradies (not "contractor" or "service professional")
- Builder (not "client" or "customer" for the demand side)
- Crew (group of tradies)
- Job (not "project" or "booking" or "task")
- Site (not "location" or "property")
- Rate (not "price" or "cost")
- Quote (not "estimate" or "proposal")
- Licence / Licenced (Australian spelling — not License/Licensed)
- Insurance (just "insurance", not "public liability insurance" in UI)
- Timesheet (one word)
- Check-in / Check-out (hyphenated)

**Avoid:**
- Professional (too formal)
- Verified expert (redundant)
- Service provider (too corporate)
- Booking (too hospitality)
- Gig (this is not Airtasker)
- Talent (this is not a recruitment platform)

## Error Messages

Always say what happened AND what to do next.

```
✓  Payment failed. Check your card details and try again.
✕  Something went wrong. Please try again later.

✓  Location access needed to find nearby tradies. Enable in Settings.
✕  We need your location to show you results.

✓  Licence photo too small. Minimum 1MB required.
✕  Upload failed. Please try a different file.
```

---

## Empty States

Never say "Nothing to see here" or use sad robots. Be factual.

```
No jobs posted yet.
Post your first job to find available tradies nearby.

No tradies match your filters.
Try removing a filter or expanding your search area.

No messages yet.
Messages from tradies will appear here.
```

---

## Notifications (Push)

Short. Specific. Actionable.

```
Marcus K. accepted your job — Site starts 7am tomorrow
New electrician available 2.4km from Fitzroy
Insurance expires in 7 days — update to stay verified
Job posted. 4 tradies notified nearby.
```

Max 60 characters for the title. Max 120 for the body.

---

## In-App Confirmation Copy

```
Post Job → "Post Job"        (not "Submit" or "Confirm")
Accept   → "Accept Job"      (not "Yes" or "Confirm booking")
Decline  → "Decline"         (not "No thanks" or "Pass")
Delete   → "Delete Job"      (not "Remove" or "Cancel")
Rate     → "Rate Marcus"     (not "Leave a review")
```

## Numbers & Formatting

| Type      | Format       | Example         |
|-----------|--------------|-----------------|

| Rating    | X.X          | 4.9             |
| Price     | $XXX/hr      | $85/hr          |
| Date      | Day Month    | 12 May          |
| Time      | Xam / X:XXpm | 7am / 2:30pm    |
| Duration  | X days / hrs | 2 days          |
| Count     | Digit always | 3 tradies, 142 jobs |
